Vision & Mission

To offer an online, easy to use, cost effective, quality guaranteed, service orientated purchasing method for individuals. Locally based service and community responsibility, coupled with purchasing convenience are the cornerstones of our business model. With all the demands on our time in today’s busy lifestyle, shopping should be a pleasure rather than a chore. Well, now it is with YOUR own online shopping store: www.wemart.pk WEmart consolidates various requirements including clothing, fashion accessories & electronics within one user friendly website. Personal service is a click away.



Our History

WEmart was founded by a fashion retailer in Pakistan in 2012, but changed to a general marketplace strategy and business model in 2015.
WEmart faces multiple of challenges along the way while competing with number of different marketplaces.
